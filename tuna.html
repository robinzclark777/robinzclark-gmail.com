Quihnn

From Quihnn's owner..."Quihnn is a lovely smart, biddable honest Golden Retriever with a wonderful temperament. He is competitive in many areas earning his Canadian Show Championship, Senior Hunter, Working Certificate Excellent, Tracking Dog Title and Urban Tracking Dog Excellent (VST). We haven’t been able to compete the last couple of years due to personal work commitments but I love living and training with this dog every day. He gets along with all dogs, lives with 2 males, he is wonderful with kids and is an affectionate loving house dog. He is well muscled, stands 23/ 1/2 inches, weighs 73lbs, has full dentition, dark eye, dark pigment with correct moderate coat of light gold. He has been completely owner trained and handled through all training in field and tracking."

This boy is lovely, smart and biddable. He loves to play and work...with swimming, hunting and tracking a the top of his list. He is honest and sweet...an absolute sweetheart to live with.
